Our Promotional Plan.

Our Plan to Record, Share, and Promote Our AI Video Effectively

Step 1

While recording

1. We will ask a catchy question to grab attention, like "Ever think about how AI can help improve your speaking and listening skills?"

2. Keep it casual, fun and not awkward.

3.We will introduce AI tools to make our points clear and to make sure our viewers can follow.

.

Step 2

Before Uploading

We will think about a catchy title that's clear and exciting.

We will design a thumbnail with pictures of AI apps or students, something that's fun and eye-catching.

We will add tags and keywords like "AI for students" so people can easily find our video.

Step 3

After Uploading

We will make sure the video is easy to find by putting it iin in a prominent spot in the homepage.

We can add a quick blog summarizing the video and explaining why people should watch it

We'll post the video link on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook

We will ask for feedbacks on our social media followers and what they think about the video and how they're using AI to improve their skills.
